  Option B (solve once, scrape many) both appear technically unavailable here,
  leaving Option C: browser automation (Playwright) that drives the form and 
  pauses for a human to enter each CAPTCHA as the only realistic, compliant
  path.

● Without touching scraper.py, the main lever is parallelism — right now it's a
  single sequential process hitting one session, and each district's response
